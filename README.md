# AI-Procurement-Operating-System
A machine learning-driven procurement and supply chain portfolio designed to improve decision quality across strategic sourcing, demand planning, inventory optimization, and supplier governance.
## Overview

This portfolio demonstrates how AI and analytics can strengthen procurement decision-making by connecting four critical capability areas:

1. **Procurement Cost Intelligence**
   - Engineering-based cost modeling
   - Quotation benchmarking
   - Cost driver transparency

2. **Demand Forecasting Engine**
   - SKU-level time-series forecasting
   - Leakage-safe backtesting
   - Forecast-driven planning signals

3. **Inventory Optimization Engine**
   - Safety stock, reorder point, EOQ, and target inventory
   - Service level vs working capital trade-off
   - Inventory policy alert layers

4. **Supplier Risk Scoring Engine**
   - Machine learning-based supplier risk classification
   - High-risk supplier identification
   - Single-source exposure alerting

## Strategic Objective

The objective of this portfolio is to demonstrate how procurement can evolve from spreadsheet-based, experience-driven decision-making toward a structured, data-enabled operating model.

Rather than treating sourcing, planning, inventory, and supplier risk as disconnected workflows, this portfolio presents them as integrated modules within an AI-enabled procurement architecture.

## Business Value

- Improve quotation transparency
- Enhance forecast-driven procurement planning
- Balance service level and working capital
- Strengthen supplier resilience governance
- Enable scalable, repeatable decision logic

## Portfolio Modules

### 1. Procurement Cost Intelligence
Supports supplier quotation benchmarking and sourcing negotiation through cost-driver analysis and should-cost logic.

### 2. Demand Forecasting Engine
Uses time-series machine learning to predict future SKU demand and improve procurement planning accuracy.

### 3. Inventory Optimization Engine
Transforms forecast uncertainty into actionable inventory policies, including safety stock, reorder points, and policy alerts.

### 4. Supplier Risk Scoring Engine
Classifies suppliers by risk level using delivery, quality, financial, operational, and geopolitical indicators.
